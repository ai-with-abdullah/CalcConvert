SIP Calculator

Calculate the future value of your Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) investments with monthly contributions.

Benefits of SIP Investment

Rupee Cost Averaging

Reduces the impact of market volatility by investing fixed amounts regularly.

Compounding Power

Your returns generate returns, accelerating wealth creation over time.

Disciplined Investing

Automates investments and builds a habit of regular saving.

Goal-Based Investing

Perfect for long-term financial goals like retirement or children's education.

How to Use the SIP Calculator

  1. 1 Enter the amount you plan to invest monthly
  2. 2 Enter the expected annual return rate (typically 10-15% for equity mutual funds)
  3. 3 Enter the investment period in years
  4. 4 Click "Calculate SIP Returns" to see your wealth accumulation projection
