Understanding Your Cycle

  • • Ovulation: Release of egg from ovary, usually mid-cycle
  • • Fertile Window: 5 days before and day of ovulation
  • • Luteal Phase: Time from ovulation to next period
  • • Best Timing: 2-3 days before ovulation for conception

⚠️ Important Note

This calculator provides estimates based on average cycles. Individual cycles may vary. Consult healthcare providers for personalized advice and family planning guidance.
