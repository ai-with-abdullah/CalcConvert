Recipe Scaling Tips

What Scales Well

  • Most liquid ingredients
  • Dry ingredients (flour, sugar)
  • Meat and main proteins
  • Vegetables and fruits

Scale with Caution

  • Salt and seasonings (taste as you go)
  • Baking powder and baking soda
  • Thickening agents (cornstarch, flour)
  • Cooking times (may not scale linearly)

How to Use the Recipe Converter

  1. 1 Enter the original number of servings your recipe makes
  2. 2 Enter the desired number of servings you want to make
  3. 3 Add your recipe ingredients with amounts and units
  4. 4 Click "Scale Recipe" to get proportionally adjusted amounts
  5. 5 Use the scaled amounts for your new serving size
