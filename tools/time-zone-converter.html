Meeting Time Suggestions

Good times for meetings between common business time zones:

US East Coast ↔ Europe

9:00 AM EST = 2:00 PM GMT

Good overlap: 8 AM - 12 PM EST

US West Coast ↔ Asia

6:00 PM PST = 10:00 AM JST+1

Good overlap: 5 PM - 9 PM PST

Europe ↔ Asia

2:00 PM CET = 6:00 PM IST

Good overlap: 1 PM - 5 PM CET

US ↔ Australia

6:00 PM PST = 1:00 PM AEDT+1

Limited overlap due to time difference

How to Use the Time Zone Converter

  1. 1 Select the time zone you want to convert from
  2. 2 Select the time zone you want to convert to
  3. 3 Enter the date and time you want to convert
  4. 4 Click "Convert Time Zone" to see the equivalent time
  5. 5 Use the world clock to see current times in major cities
