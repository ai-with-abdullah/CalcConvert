Understanding ROI

  • • ROI = (Final Value - Initial Investment) / Initial Investment × 100
  • • Positive ROI indicates profit, negative ROI indicates loss
  • • Compare ROI across different investments to make informed decisions
  • • Consider time period - longer investments may have higher ROI requirements
  • • Factor in inflation and opportunity costs for accurate analysis
