Travel Planning Tips

Time Buffers

  • Add 10-15% extra time for unexpected delays
  • Account for rush hour traffic
  • Consider weather conditions
  • Plan breaks every 2-3 hours

Long Distance Tips

  • Check route for construction zones
  • Plan fuel stops in advance
  • Download offline maps as backup
  • Consider overnight stops for very long trips

How to Use the Travel Time Calculator

  1. 1 Choose whether to calculate travel time or arrival time
  2. 2 Enter the total distance of your journey
  3. 3 Enter your expected average speed
  4. 4 If calculating arrival time, enter departure date and time
  5. 5 Add any additional time for breaks, fuel stops, or delays
