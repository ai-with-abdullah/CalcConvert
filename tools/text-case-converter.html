Text Case Converter

Convert text between different cases: uppercase, lowercase, title case, camel case, and more.

Case Conversion Examples

UPPERCASE
"hello world" → "HELLO WORLD"
lowercase
"HELLO WORLD" → "hello world"
Title Case
"hello world" → "Hello World"
camelCase
"hello world" → "helloWorld"
PascalCase
"hello world" → "HelloWorld"
snake_case
"hello world" → "hello_world"

How to Use the Text Case Converter

  1. 1 Enter or paste your text in the input area
  2. 2 Click on the desired case conversion button
  3. 3 The converted text will appear in the output area
  4. 4 Use the copy button to copy the converted text
