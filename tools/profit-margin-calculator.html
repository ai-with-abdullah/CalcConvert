Key Formulas

Profit Margin

Margin = (Selling Price - Cost) ÷ Selling Price × 100

Markup

Markup = (Selling Price - Cost) ÷ Cost × 100

Gross Profit

Gross Profit = Selling Price - Cost Price

Break-even Price

Break-even = Cost ÷ (1 - Desired Margin%)

How to Use the Profit Margin Calculator
