Break-Even Analysis

  • • Break-Even Point = Fixed Costs ÷ (Selling Price - Variable Cost)
  • • Units above break-even point generate profit
  • • Lower variable costs or higher selling prices improve break-even
  • • Essential for pricing strategy and business planning
  • • Use for product launches and investment decisions
